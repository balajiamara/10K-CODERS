ALLU ARJUN

Allu Arjun is a celebrated Indian film actor known for his dynamic performances and exceptional dancing skills in Telugu cinema. Often called the "Stylish Star," he has delivered back to back blockbusters like Pushpa: The Rule, Pushpa: The Rise and Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo , earning him pan-India fame. He has won several prestigious awards, including multiple Filmfare Awards South and a National Film Award. His charismatic screen presence and dedication have made him one of the most influential actors in the industry.

69th National Film Awards


Telugu actor Allu Arjun has become the first Telugu actor to win the best actor award at the National Film Awards. He received the award for his role in the Telugu film 'Pushpa: The Rise'.
